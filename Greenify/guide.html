Going green can be tough, especially when you’re starting out. There are endless things to remember and it can be hard to keep track. That’s why we’ve put together these general guidelines that are easy to memorise and applicable in many different situations

Recycle

Recycle everything you possibly can, all plastic, metal, paper and glass. In most places there are recycling bins in every neighbourhood. By reducing air and water pollution and saving energy, recycling offers an important environmental benefit: it reduces emissions of greenhouse gases, such as carbon dioxide, methane, nitrous oxide and chlorofluorocarbons, that contribute to global climate change.
